Explain¶

Given the presence of outliers and skew, along with the overall spacing—or lack thereof—transforming the data may help with visualizing it. Most of the scores are right around 500 (±25).

In [24]:
len(df_tot_2012.query('Value >= 475 and Value <= 525')) / len(df_tot_2012)
Out[24]:
0.7435897435897436

And most of the outliers are low—not high (see graphs above). This calls for a transformation that will space out the glut of larger numbers while bringing the smaller outliers into field.
