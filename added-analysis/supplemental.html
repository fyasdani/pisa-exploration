Explain¶

Given the presence of outliers and skew, along with the overall spacingor lack thereoftransforming the data may help with visualizing it. Most of the scores are right around 500 (25).

And most of the outliers are lownot high (see graphs above). This calls for a transformation that will space out the glut of larger numbers while bringing the smaller outliers into field.

exp = 3  # determine the spacing
exp_score = np.power(df_tot_2012_sort['Value'], exp)

plt.figure(figsize=(10, 6))
ax = sns.barplot(x=df_tot_2012_sort['Country'], y=exp_score, color='b')
